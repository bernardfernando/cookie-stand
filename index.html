Pat's passion for Salmon

Pat loves sweets and has a passion for healthy ecosystems. This led him to develop a recipe for a coffee-time confection called Salmon Cookies. These cookies made into the shape of a salmon and are suitable for both humans and salmon to enjoy. At present they are available in Seattle USA, Tokyo, Dubai, Paris and Lima. Soon they will be available near you too!
